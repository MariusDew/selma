Be better

So you want a better life, do you? then follow these tips.
To get a better life you have to be healthy both mentally and physically. these 2 are linked togheter and having the one makes the other one much easier to obtain and maintain.
We have the best tips to help u with both of these.

how to look better

being proud of how you look is an important step towards mental health. but this does mean you have to look perfect to be happy.
many people focus too much on how they look and no matter how good you might look that is not healthy.
the main focus isnt on looking the best, but on accepting how you look and being proud of the body you have.
working out and taking care of skin, hair and hygiene are good ways to feel better about yourself and how you look

how to eat healthier

how you eat affects both mind and body. and one meal can be the difference between hitting a new personal goal, or staying in bed doing nothing
eating healthy does not mean cutting out all sugar and fats. eating healthy is about getting the right balance of everything.
an easy way to change your eating habits to be a little healthier is to switch out snacks in the normal week day with different fruits and you can add more fruit in your lunchbox
when eating meat eat unprocessed meat straight from the animal.
eat more whole grains and different types of nuts. these are high in many important nutriants.
you can also search up resipies for healthier foods and maybe you will even find a new favorite food.

how to sleep better

sleep might be the most important step towards a better life but also the hardest one. but it being hard doesnt mean its impossible
how to get better sleep varies from person to person but these are some tips that help most people.
sleep at the same time every night. this lets your body recoginze when its supposed to go to sleep.
have a good room temperature. what roomtemperature works best can vary but your room shouldnt be too cold or too hot.
having a room only for sleeping makes your brain connect this room with sleep and therefore it falls asleep faster

how to walk better

have a good posture

how to smell better

smelling good is important for both you and other people so this is an important one.
just take a shower and put on deodorant and you should be good.
also brush your teeth atleast twice a day and remember to floss. good breath is important too.

how to talk better

just dont overthink it and your good

how to sit better

sit upright and dont sit too much
